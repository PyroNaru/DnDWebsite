Incident Report: Case File 0412-AE

Visual Record:

Photo of AshGrave.

Incident Name: The Starfall Aberration

Filed By: Ashgrave Investigation Team

Report Class: AE

Threat Classification: HIGH, Formerly Unknown

Required Clearance: Level 2

Status: Finished

Last Updated: October 1973

Description:

Reports have surfaced from the mountain village of Ashgrave regarding mysterious and unsettling phenomena. Initial claims describe missing persons, cracked religious statues, and erratic behavior among the villagers. Local officials, including Mayor Edrick Vael, are unaccounted for. Multiple search parties sent to investigate have either failed to return or provided nonsensical reports.

Accounts suggest something far beyond comprehension may be at work. Unexplained mutations, strange lights, and whispers heard in the darkness point to a possible connection to celestial or aberrant forces. With no concrete understanding of the source, an investigation team is assembling to uncover the truth behind Ashgrave’s plight.
