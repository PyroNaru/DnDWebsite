File-001 — The Whispering Vein

Classification: Parasitica-Class

Required Clearance: Level 2 (Custos)

Status: Active Containment

Last Updated: October 2025

Description:

Subject-001, designated ██████████, is a vascular anomaly discovered beneath the catacombs of █████████. Its organic structure pulsates in rhythm with seismic fluctuations detected up to 300 kilometers away. Auditory phenomena reported by research personnel include whispers in archaic Latin, referencing an entity called “Sanguis Primus.”

Exposure to frequencies emitted by the Vein has resulted in psychological deterioration, hemophobia, and in some cases, vascular crystallization. Personnel assigned must undergo daily psychological evaluation and monthly detoxification protocols.

Containment Protocol:

  • Containment chamber must remain at -4°C at all times.
  • Access limited to personnel with Custos-level clearance or higher.
  • Audio recording devices are prohibited within 20 meters of the chamber.

Addendum 001-A:

Following Incident 001-A, any research regarding resonance frequencies is suspended. Agent Lira’s final log suggests the Vein communicates through rhythmic blood flow patterns, implying sentient biological encoding.
