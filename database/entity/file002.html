File-002 — Hollow Caller

Visual Record:

None

Threat Level: Code Crimson

Required Clearance: Level 2

Status: Unknown

Last Updated: March 1975

Description:

The "Hollow Caller", referred by the notes found by agent Evet and Guiliana, is a humanoid shapeshifting like entity. it mimicked the appearances of the agents of an impromptu investiagetion of the countryside town.

The creature, according to notes that were recovered, has the ability to shapeshift into creatures it has consumed. However, it was able to shapeshift into unstable versions of agents despite our agents have no recollection of them being consumed. It also able to recall and relay memories of the creatures it has temporarily shapeshift into.

Containment Protocol:

  • None as of late, if seen do not engage alone and contact HQ

Addendum 001-A:

Following Incident stated as in Incident Report: Case File 0345-AE, any news or sightings of the creature must be reported immediately before following containment procedure.
