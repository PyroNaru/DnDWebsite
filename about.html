About Global Occult and Obscurity Neutralization

The Lex Obscura Initiative (L.O.I) is a multinational covert directive established to locate, classify, and contain phenomena that threaten the veil of human normalcy.

Founded in the aftermath of the “Red Sky Event,” the Initiative’s mandate is to study the unseen and enforce the silence. All recovered entities, objects, or individuals are to be documented within the Archives.

“Ignorance preserves sanity. Knowledge demands containment.”
