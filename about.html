About Global Occult and Obscurity Neutralization

The Global Occult and Obscurity Neutralization (G.O.O.N) is a secretive directive established to locate, classify, and contain phenomena that threaten the veil of the city's normalcy.

Founded during the early days of "New Omphalos" the Initiative’s mandate is to study the unseen and enforce the silence. All recovered entities, objects, or individuals are to be documented within the Archives.

“Secure, Contain, Protect.”
